FAQs

What is IEPF share recovery?

When shares or dividends remain unclaimed, they may be transferred to the Investor Education and Protection Fund (IEPF). Recovery involves filing a claim with supporting documents to restore them to the rightful owner or legal heir.

What is DEAF for bank deposits?

Unclaimed amounts in bank accounts may move to the Depositor Education and Awareness Fund (DEAF). We help trace accounts and process claims with the bank.

Do you provide PAN/GST registration support?

Yes. We handle fresh registrations, amendments, return filing, and routine compliance.

Can you prepare project reports for bank loans?

Yes. We prepare detailed project reports with financials, ratios, and projections tailored to lender requirements.
